What is 3rd Position SMP?

3rd Position SMP (better known as 3TP SMP in short) is a normal survival multiplayer server with 3rd positionist influences. While keeping the core experience rooted in vanilla Minecraft survival, our community incorporates structural elements of factional coordination, unique group governance models, and distinct ideological themes. It is a world where regular base-building meets political theory, allowing groups to establish organized territories while enjoying standard cooperative gameplay.
